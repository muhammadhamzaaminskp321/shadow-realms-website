🎮 Shadow Realms: Rise of the Forgotten

An RPG where story, combat, and consequence collide.

🔥 About the Project

In a world where magical realms collide and ancient curses rise, many fantasy RPGs focus too much on either combat or story. Shadow Realms: Rise of the Forgotten brings balance to both.

As a Forgotten One, a resurrected warrior, you'll explore cursed ruins, forge alliances or rivalries, and ultimately shape the fate of the Shadow Realm. With deep character builds, branching storylines, and real choice-driven gameplay, this is more than just a game — it's your saga.
