Shadow Realms: Rise of the Forgotten

About the Project

In a time when magical realms and ancient curses collide, Shadow Realms: Rise of the Forgotten brings a fresh and balanced take on the RPG genre. Many fantasy games today lean too heavily on either combat or story, but few manage to strike a meaningful balance between the two.

Our game solves this by offering a deep narrative, rich lore, and dynamic combat — all within an open world where your choices truly matter. You'll play as a Forgotten One, a resurrected warrior navigating cursed ruins, forging alliances or rivalries, and shaping the destiny of the Shadow Realm.
